### Section 5: Feature Priorities

**10. Imagine a new "Study App." Please rank the following features from 1 (Most Useful) to 5 (Least Useful):**
*(In Google Forms, you can use a "Grid" or "Ranking" question type)*

*   **Unified Search:** Search everything (lectures, labs, notes) in one place.
*   **Chat with Notes (RAG):** Ask questions specifically about *your* uploaded documents.
*   **Auto-Quizzes:** Generate practice questions instantly from your lecture slides.
*   **Auto-Flashcards:** Convert lecture slides into flashcards automatically.
*   **Calendar Sync:** Automatically extract deadlines from module descriptors to your calendar.
