# Product Guide: StudySpace

## Initial Concept
**StudySpace** is an AI-powered "Study Operating System" designed to centralize and enhance the undergraduate learning experience. It solves the problem of fragmented academic materials by providing a single platform for ingestion, organization, and active study.

## Vision
To build the definitive "Study Hub" for **all undergraduate students**, regardless of discipline. StudySpace transforms a chaotic collection of PDFs, slides, and notes into an organized, intelligent knowledge base that not only stores information but actively helps students learn through retrieval practice and spaced repetition.

## Core Value Proposition
*   **Unified Organization ("The Shoebox"):** Stop wasting time managing folders. Upload everything (PDF, PPTX, DOCX) and let the system classify it by module and resource type.
*   **Intelligent Tutor:** Chat with your materials using grounded AI (RAG) that cites sources, ensuring trust and reducing hallucinations.
*   **Active Recall:** Automatically generate quizzes and flashcards from your course content to support evidence-based learning techniques.

## Target Audience
*   **Primary:** Undergraduate students across all disciplines (Humanities, Business, STEM).
*   **Secondary:** Lifelong learners and researchers managing large document corpora.

## Key Features
1.  **Smart Ingestion Pipeline:**
    *   Support for PDF, DOCX, PPTX, and Images.
    *   OCR for scanned notes and diagrams.
    *   Auto-classification of modules (e.g., "History 101", "Calculus").
2.  **RAG-Powered Chat:**
    *   Context-aware answers grounded in user documents.
    *   Citation transparency (click-to-view source).
3.  **Active Study Tools:**
    *   **Quiz Engine:** Generate multiple-choice and short-answer quizzes from specific lectures.
    *   **Flashcard Generator:** Create spaced-repetition cards from key terms and definitions.
4.  **Administrative Intelligence:**
    *   Extract deadlines and assessment weightings from module descriptors.
    *   Calendar synchronization (iCal/Google Calendar).

## Success Metrics
*   **Efficiency:** Reduction in time spent organizing files and searching for specific information.
*   **Learning Quality:** Accuracy of generated answers/quizzes and student engagement with active study tools.
*   **User Trust:** High citation accuracy and low hallucination rates in RAG responses.

## Deployment Strategy
*   **Cloud / Hosted Web Application:** Accessible from any device (laptop, tablet, phone) via a standard web browser, with server-side processing for heavy NLP tasks.
